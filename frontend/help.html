User Guide

How to Use the System

1. Logging In

  1. Open the system by navigating to login.html in your browser.
  2. Enter your Username and Password.
  3. Click the Login button.
  4. On success, you will be taken to the Dashboard.
  5. Default credentials: admin / admin123 or staff / staff123.

2. Adding a New Reservation

  1. From the Dashboard, click New Reservation.
  2. Fill in all required fields marked with a red asterisk (*).
  3. Enter the guest name (letters and spaces only, minimum 2 characters).
  4. Enter a valid contact number (10-15 digits).
  5. Select the appropriate room type from the dropdown.
  6. Choose a check-in date (today or future) and a check-out date (after check-in).
  7. Click Create Reservation. A unique reservation number is automatically assigned.

3. Viewing All Reservations

  1. Click All Reservations from the Dashboard.
  2. A table showing all reservations will be displayed.
  3. Use the Search box at the top to filter by guest name, reservation number, or contact number.
  4. Click View next to any reservation to see its full details.

4. Editing a Reservation

  1. Open a reservation by clicking View on the reservations list.
  2. Click the Edit Reservation button.
  3. Update the fields you want to change.
  4. Click Save Changes to apply the update.
  5. The system will recalculate the total amount automatically if dates or room type change.

5. Deleting a Reservation

  1. Open a reservation by clicking View, then click Delete Reservation.
  2. Alternatively, click the Delete button directly in the reservations table.
  3. A confirmation dialog will appear — click OK to confirm deletion.
  4. Warning: Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

6. Calculating and Printing a Bill

  1. Click Calculate Bill from the Dashboard.
  2. Select the reservation from the dropdown list.
  3. The bill will be displayed showing the room type, number of nights, price per night, and total amount.
  4. Click Print Bill to print the bill for the guest.

7. Logging Out

  1. Click the Logout button in the top-right corner at any time.
  2. You will be redirected to the login page.
  3. Always log out when leaving the workstation unattended.
